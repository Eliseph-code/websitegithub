About me

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by beautiful imagery and the intricate details found in nature.

As a child, I spent hours drawing objects and scenes that inspired me, and I loved experimenting with simple materials to create functional items.













That early passion for craftsmanship and design has always stayed with me.

I chose to study interior design as my first formal education – a deliberate decision to combine my technical understanding with creative expression.

Throughout my studies, I developed a strong sense of form and space, and discovered how much I enjoy bringing ideas to life.

Alongside my education, I also began crafting handmade objects, such as leather bags, allowing me to explore design on a more personal level.

These experiences have led me to what I truly love doing: designing and building.

This passion ultimately inspired me to take the step into the world of architecture.

– a field where creativity, technical insight, and meaningful impact come together –
